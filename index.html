A Scenic & Peaceful Sanctuary

Welcome to Song River Homestay

Tucked away in the quiet, green neighborhood of Cay Gia Ha inside Thanh Dong Hamlet (Cam Thanh, Hoi An), Song River Homestay presents a refreshing oasis of calm. Our home sits right alongside peaceful local waterways, located just 2.6 km away from the historic Ancient Town.

When you stay here, you are treated like family by our wonderful local hosts, Ms. Hiep and Mr. Binh. We designed our rooms with simple, sparkling clean layouts and spacious modern bathrooms. Every morning, wake up to a freshly prepared Vietnamese specialty breakfast served right on our garden patio.

Q&A

Frequently Asked Questions

Check-in is welcome from 14:00 to 23:00 daily. Check-out is scheduled from 06:00 to 12:00. If you require early check-in or late check-out options, please let us know in advance and we will accommodate based on room availability.
To maintain convenient family operations, Song River Homestay accepts **Cash payments (VND or USD)** upon arrival. We currently do not support direct credit card swipes at reception.
To maintain safety and a peaceful evening environment for everyone, our main gate is securely locked between **23:00 and 06:00** daily. If you plan to explore late, please coordinate with us so we can receive you smoothly!
Unfortunately, to ensure high cleanliness standards and accommodate guests with allergies or young kids, pets are not permitted in our rooms. Thank you for your understanding!
